# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the CodeMantis community a welcoming and positive experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Gracefully accepting feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
